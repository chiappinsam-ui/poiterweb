Our story

Built on red rock. Finished like marble.

St. George Concrete started in 2009 with one mixer truck and a promise: every slab we pour gets treated like it's going in our own backyard. Fifteen years later, that promise hasn't changed — just the size of the crew keeping it.

We batch and cure for our desert climate, hand-finish every decorative detail, and stand behind the work with a craftsmanship warranty most contractors won't offer.
